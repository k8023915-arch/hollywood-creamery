🌟 Our Story

From the Lens
to the Scoop

📸 🍦 🎨

Hollywood Creamery was born in 2000 when Konstantin Goldenberg — a local photographer known for his eye for detail and unique creative vision — decided it was time for a new chapter.

After years of capturing the beauty in everyday moments, Konstantin turned his artistic instincts toward something equally personal: handcrafting small-batch ice cream from scratch, experimenting with bold and unexpected flavors, and sharing the joy of that very first taste with everyone who walks through the door.

Here in South Florida, where people come from every corner of the world, Hollywood Creamery became his way of connecting with the community — one scoop, one smile, one unforgettable flavor at a time.
