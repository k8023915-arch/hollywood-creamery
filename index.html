🌟 Our Story

A Sweet Tradition,
Generation by Generation

🍦 🍓 🍫 🌿

Hollywood Creamery was born from a simple belief: ice cream should be made the right way — by hand, in small batches, with the finest ingredients, and shared with the people you love.

We're a family business rooted in the heart of Hollywood, Florida. Every flavor we craft carries that tradition forward, one scoop at a time.

Come find us inside Block 40 Food Hall — and bring the whole family! 🎉
